Website Proposal: Personal Portfolio

Topic

My website will be a personal portfolio that showcases my skills, projects, and experiences. It will serve as an online resume and a platform to display my work.

Audience

The intended audience for my personal portfolio includes potential employers, clients, and anyone interested in learning about my skills and projects.

Design and Style

The design will be clean, modern, and visually appealing. The color scheme will include a primary color of #64636 (Gray) and in order to keep it simple, I will use black and white.

Website Inspirations for the design include:

  • Pallas

  • Jacky Winter

  • Keita Yamada Portfolio

    • Scope

    The website will have a landing-page with the following sections as sub-pages: Home, About Me, Skills, Projects, Experiences, and Contact. Each section will have substantial content that caa be linked to their own page.
